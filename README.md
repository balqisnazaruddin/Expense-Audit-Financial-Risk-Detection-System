# Internal Expense Audit & Financial Risk Detection Framework

## 📌 Business Case & Problem
Manual data entries and unsanitized operational logs left the company vulnerable to financial leakages, duplicate payouts, and undocumented vendor expenses.

## 🛠️ Technical Solution & Tooling
* **Core Stack:** Microsoft Excel (Advanced Data Cleaning, Power Query, Relational Schemas, Tabular Data Structuring)
* **The Fix:** Transformed chaotic operational records into a highly structured database to audit regional expenditures and isolate financial anomalies.

## 🎯 Quantifiable Business Impact
* **Data Sanitation & Schema Design:** Cleansed and structured a chaotic operational database worth RM67,328.82 into a clean, query-ready tabular format.
* **Risk Mitigation & Audit Trail:** Implemented an automated classification system categorized by risk levels (*Verified*, *Needs Vendor Detail*, *Large Repair Check*) to detect financial anomalies and stop budget leakages.
* **Compliance Ready:** Streamlined internal tracking to ensure the company's financial records are fully corporate tax-ready.
